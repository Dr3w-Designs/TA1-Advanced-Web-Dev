Malico Year End Trail

October 30, 2025

The Malico Year End Trail marked a significant milestone in the 2025 season, bringing together the community for a comprehensive test of all the skills they'd developed throughout the year. This event showcased the diversity of Enduro Trail Ubusan's terrain and the progression riders have made.

Malico's varied landscape presented challenges across multiple disciplines—sustained climbs, technical descents, obstacle navigation, and endurance sections. Riders had to be versatile, switching mindsets and techniques as they transitioned between different terrain types. This variety is what makes Malico such a beloved fixture in the calendar.

The year-end nature of the event added an extra layer of significance. Participants used it as an opportunity to reflect on their achievements, identify areas for growth, and set intentions for the coming year. The trails became a canvas for personal transformation and community celebration.

As riders crossed the finish line at Malico, many felt a sense of accomplishment that extended far beyond the race results. They had tested themselves against challenging terrain, supported fellow riders, and been part of something larger than themselves—the Enduro Trail Ubusan family.
