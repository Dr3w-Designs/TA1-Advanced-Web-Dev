Clearing Boy Yabang Uphill

November 15, 2025

The Clearing Boy Yabang Uphill challenge stands as one of the most demanding segments in the Enduro Trail Ubusan circuit. This brutal ascent tests not only the physical capabilities of riders but also their mental fortitude. With steep gradients and technical terrain, it's a true measure of endurance and determination.

During this particular event, riders faced ideal conditions for such a grueling challenge. The ground was firm, offering decent traction despite the incline. However, the altitude and sustained effort required left many gasping for breath at the summit. Yet, one by one, determined riders conquered the climb, each celebrating their own personal victory.

What's remarkable about the Clearing Boy Yabang course is how it tests every aspect of a rider's abilities. Power, endurance, technique, and mental resilience are all required to complete this section.
